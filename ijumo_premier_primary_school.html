A School Built on
Purpose & Community

Founded in the heart of Mitooma, Ijumo Premier Primary School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Western Uganda. We combine the best of the Uganda National Curriculum with enriched co-curricular activities to develop the whole child.

How to Enrol Your Child

The admissions process is simple and transparent. We admit new pupils at the start of each term, subject to availability.

1

Visit or Call the School

Come to our campus in Mitooma or call us to express your interest. Our admissions team will give you a warm welcome and answer all your questions.

2

Collect & Submit the Admissions Form

Pick up an admissions form at the school office (or download it below). Fill in the child's details, attach copies of the previous school report card and birth certificate.

3

Assessment & Placement Interview

Your child will have a brief, friendly assessment so we can place them in the most suitable class and tailor support from day one.

4

Pay Fees & Confirm Enrolment

Once accepted, pay the first-term fees, collect the school uniform list, and your child is ready to start their journey at Ijumo Premier!
